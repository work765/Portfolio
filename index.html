About

16 years of
art direction.

Art director and graphic designer based in Denver, Colorado, with a career built across entertainment studios, agencies and tech companies — and most recently as founder of ElectroPop, a direct-to-consumer electrolyte lollipop brand built and run solo from the ground up.

The work spans theatrical key art for major studios, packaging and brand development for consumer products, and web design for enterprise clients — always with the same instinct: concept first, craft second, and never settle for the obvious layout.
