Art director and graphic designer based in Denver, Colorado. Currently founder of ElectroPop, a direct-to-consumer electrolyte lollipop brand built and run solo — product development, packaging, brand identity, paid media, fulfillment and everything in between.

Before that, in-house and freelance design leadership for OneTrust, Microsoft's Windows.com and Ring, plus consumer brands like QUANTUM Energy Squares — print and digital collateral, packaging, landing pages and paid campaigns built to perform, not just look good.
